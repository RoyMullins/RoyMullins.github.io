Who are we?

Dhrubajyoti Roy

Chair Applicant, Rashtradhykash of Vishwamitra

With over 14 years of experience, Dhrub is one of the most experienced micronationalists within the Cupertino Alliance. Studying Political Science and History, he knowe what makes a good leader, and he has executed his role within Vishwamitra to his best ability and effort. He has proven himself to be dedicated to the alliance, serving his role as Deputy Minister of Asian and Oceanic Affairs flawlessly, always able to host sessions when others can't.

Tyler Mullins

Lieutenant Chair Applicant, King of New Athens

Having already served the Cupertino Alliance for almost a year, and being part of the Bureau d'Administration for six months, Mullins has shown himself to be worthy of the responsibilities of Lt. Chair. Furthermore, he is one of the highest-ranking officials within the League of Independent Nations, serving as the Mediator-General. He is always at LIN meetings on-time, and is able to efficiently schedule and manage meetings. He is incredibly well put-together, and a very friendly figure in the community.
